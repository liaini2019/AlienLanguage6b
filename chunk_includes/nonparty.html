Grouping activity

When you arrive on the planet you will begin your mission by hosting a diplomatic gathering.

It’s very important that you don’t make any faux pas!

It’s important, for instance, that you are aware of the social distinctions on Ginuli, in particular between the different species and between the different flowers that aliens wear.

If we don’t make sure to learn these distinctions, it will be a catastrophe!

On the next few screens we’ll practice making social distinctions.
