Grouping activity

When you arrive on the planet you will begin your mission by hosting a diplomatic gathering.

It’s very important that you don’t make any faux pas!

It’s important, for instance, that you are aware of the social distinctions on Ginuli

We have to be very careful who we invite and do not invite.

On Ginuli, status is closely connected with the flower that you wear.

Blue flowers are harder to obtain and are a sign of political importance.

They are only worn by the most senior aliens.

If we don’t make sure to invite these aliens to the gathering, it will be a catastrophe!

On the next few screens we’ll practice making social distinctions.
